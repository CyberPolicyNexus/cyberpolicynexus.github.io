About the Nexus

The Cyber Policy Nexus (or just the Nexus) is a Slack workspace for cyber policy professionals from around the world to congregate and collaborate.

I know what you're thinking, "Not another Slack?!" But hear me out. The cyber policy community lacks a common online space to educate each other and share expertise and ideas. Slack provides a platform for open discussions on general topics and allows individual groups to start their own private channels for private conversations or to explore specific topics.

Participation in the Nexus is contingent on the Rules of Engagement. If you have a cyber policy role and agree to uphold the Rules of Engagement, register through the Signup Form below.
